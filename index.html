Your public IP address that show in CMYIP is a unique numerical identifier assigned to a device, such as a computer, phone or router, that is connected to a network and accessible over the internet. It allows devices to communicate with other devices and services on the internet. Public IP addresses are globally unique and can be used to identify and locate devices on the internet. They are provided by Internet Service Providers (ISPs) and are visible to other devices on the internet, enabling communication and data transfer between them.
